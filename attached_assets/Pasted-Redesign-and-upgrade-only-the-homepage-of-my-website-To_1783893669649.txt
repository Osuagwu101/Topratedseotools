Redesign and upgrade only the homepage of my website, Top Rated SEO Tools, to make it look professional, trustworthy, modern, conversion-focused, and capable of convincing visitors to purchase.

Website: https://topratedseotools.replit.app/

Do not redesign the entire website or remove any existing backend functionality. Focus on the homepage while preserving the current authentication, payment, customer dashboard, product, subscription, admin, and Paystack systems.

## Main objective

The homepage should no longer look like a simple list of tools. It should function as a professional sales landing page that:

* Clearly explains what Top Rated SEO Tools offers.
* Shows why customers should buy from us.
* Builds trust.
* Explains how the purchasing process works.
* Highlights selected popular tools.
* Directs visitors to the full Catalogue page.
* Encourages visitors to purchase or contact support.
* Looks excellent on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

Move the complete list of tools away from the homepage and place it on the Catalogue page. However, retain a “Popular Tools” section on the homepage showing only four to six selected tools.

The Admin should be able to select which tools appear as popular or featured tools from the backend.
